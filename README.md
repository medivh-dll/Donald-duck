## 🧠 What It Does

Every time you hit **MORE WISDOM**, the page generates a brand-new, never-before-seen Trump-style quote by randomly combining:

- **Openers** — *"Many people are saying —"*, *"Nobody talks about this, but"*
- **Middles** — riffs on covfefe, crowd size, tariffs, Epstein's list, and more
- **Closers** — *"Believe me."*, *"Sad!"*, *"MAGA."*
- **Topics & People** — randomized fill-ins for infinite variety

Quotes are delivered with a **typewriter animation** and can be **tweeted directly** with one click.

Also features a scrolling news ticker with important breaking headlines.
